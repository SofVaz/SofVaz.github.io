I’m a second year PhD student at LAMSADE, at Université Paris Dauphine supervised by Cristina Bazgan. My research focuses on Community Detection in Networks from the algorithmic and graph-theoretical point of view. I am particularly interested in understanding the Computational Complexity of different community detection problems, and designing efficient algorithms to solve them.

I like all things graph-related, and love combinatorial arguments.
